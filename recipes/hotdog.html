Hotdog

Description

This recipe will walk you through how to make one of the easiest foods maybe ever. We will need a pan and a stovetop.

Ingredients

  • Hotdog
  • Hotdog Bun
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Put your pan on your stovetop and turn the heat to medium. Let the pan heat up a little before cooking.
  2. Once pan is hot, add just the hotdog onto the pan.
  3. Let the hotdog cook until it starts to darken while turning it often. This should take about 7 minutes.
  4. Prepare your hotdog bun on a plate, opening them up.
  5. Remove the hotdog from the pan and put it into the bun.
  6. Add your ketchup and mustard, and enjoy!

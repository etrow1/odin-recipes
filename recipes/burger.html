Hamburger

This recipe teaches you how to cook a Hamburger. You will need a cooking pan and

Ingredients

  • Store bought frozen pizza bread
  • Pepperoni
  • Rao's Pizza Sauce
  • Mozzarella (shredded)

Steps

  1. Thaw out your frozen pizza bread.
  2. Preheat your oven to 425 F
  3. Slap all your ingredients onto the pizza bread in the following order.
    1. Pizza Sauce
    2. Cheese
    3. Pepperoni
  4. Once the oven is preheated, throw the pizza directly onto the middle rack in the oven. Let cook for 25 minutes.
  5. Pull pizza out of the oven. No idea the best way to do this with it being directly on the rack. Good luck!
  6. Cut the pizza into whatever shape you please, and enjoy!

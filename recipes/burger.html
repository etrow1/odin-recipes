Hamburger

Description

This recipe teaches you how to cook a Hamburger. You will need a cooking pan and a stove top.

Ingredients

  • Raw Hamburger Circles
  • Hamburger Buns
  • American Cheese
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonnaise
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Put your pan on your stovetop and turn the heat to medium. Let the pan heat up a little before cooking.
  2. Once the pan is hot, oil the pan with some vegetable oil. Place the hamburger onto the pan.
  3. Cook the hamburger for 5 minutes and then flip it to the other side.
  4. Prepare your bun on a plate. Once the hamburger has reached the appropriate internal temperature, remove it from the pan and place it on the bun.
  5. Place all of your toppings on the burger in any order you would like! Enjoy!
